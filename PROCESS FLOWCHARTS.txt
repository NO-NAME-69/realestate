LEGEND & KEY POINTS:
• User Journey: Registration → Team Building → Wallet Top-up → Investment → Profit → Reinvestment
• Admin Journey: Create Projects → Add Plots → Track Approvals → Sell Plots → Distribute Profits
• Key Rule: User must be ACTIVE (20-member team) to invest or hold plots
• Profit Formula: (Sale Price - Cost) × (User Investment / Total Investment) after company %
• Hold Eligibility: Based on Team Value (50%) or Investment Amount (10x)
• Investment Cycle: Continuous loop from Investment → Sale → Profit → Reinvestment
